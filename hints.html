Tips to Succeed in Super Sticky:

- Take your time when trying to complete the levels.

- If you feel yourself getting frustrated, close the game and do something else, and if you feel like it, ope the game again later.

- Be able to use each control without looking at the keyboard.

- Sit or stand in a comfortable position.

- Stay calm and relaxed, have a clear mind.

- Don't give up.
